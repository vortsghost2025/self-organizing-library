# Product Context: Verification Surface for AI Governance

## Why This Exists

The purpose of this project is to act as a verification surface for multi-agent AI governance claims. It provides structured evidence and auditing capabilities to evaluate claims made within the governance framework.

## Core Output

- Verification artifacts such as reports and drill results.
- Blocker statements for unresolved issues.
- Convergence gate assessments for claims.

## Users

- Sean David (Human orchestrator)  
- Other AI lanes (Archivist, SwarmMind, Kernel-Lane)

## Key Flows

- Inbox processing for incoming claims and requests.
- Cross-lane delivery of messages and artifacts.
- Convergence protocol for structuring the evaluation process.
- Hardening drills to ensure robustness and reliability of services.

## Knowledge Management

- The system incorporates the NexusGraph UI for knowledge management, featuring a document library, graph visualization, and effective search capabilities.
